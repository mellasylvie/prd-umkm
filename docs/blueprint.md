# **App Name**: UMKM Digital Hub

## Core Features:

- Minisite Builder: Create a simple, professional minisite using pre-designed templates with easy customization options (logo, banner, business info, maps, CTA buttons).
- Digital Catalog: Add and edit product listings with photos, prices, descriptions, and categories. Manage stock status and display products in grid or list views.
- Automated Promo Poster Generator: Automatically generate promotional posters with pre-made templates that can be shared to social media. The LLM acts as a tool that can provide a relevant message associated with a promotion for each UMKM.
- One-Click Sharing: Share the minisite link and generated promotional content directly to WhatsApp, Instagram, and Facebook with a single click.
- Basic Analytics Dashboard: Track key metrics such as the number of visits, most viewed products, and CTA clicks (WhatsApp).
- UMKM Account Management: Easy registration and profile management for UMKM, including name, address, and business category. Support multi-device login.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft, earthy green (#A7D1AB) to evoke a sense of growth and reliability for UMKM businesses.
- Background color: Very light desaturated green (#F0F4EF) for a clean and calming backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted teal (#79B4B7) to add a touch of sophistication and modernity.
- Font pairing: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for headlines to convey a geometric, modern feel; 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for body text to ensure readability and a humanist touch.
- Clean and simple icons to represent different product categories and actions. Icons should be easily understandable and contribute to the overall user experience.
- Use a grid-based layout to ensure content is well-organized and easy to navigate. Prioritize key information above the fold and maintain a consistent design throughout the platform.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user interaction and provide feedback without being distracting. For example, smooth loading animations or hover effects.
